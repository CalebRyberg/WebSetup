Restaraunt Description

Welcome to Classico`! You guessed it, here is where you will endulge in classic recipes of America. Did we throw you off, did you think this was an elegant Italian restaurant? You're not alone... that is one of our many intelligent marketing aspects. We do however pride ourselves on our classics like the Hot Dog and Burger. These may be tasty dishes but a bit heavy, in which case our House Salad will do the trick. If you are feeling for something a little different be sure to try our Tenderloin Sandwich which is always sure to satisfy. Don't leave before trying one of our in-house brews as they have one many awards throughout the Midwest and have been on many TV shows such as Brewmaster's. Enjoy our subtle dinner atmosphere or travel over to the bar to catch the game. Either way you look at it, Classico` is a classic!
